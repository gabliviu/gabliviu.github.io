Smooth, rich, aromatic

montecillo blend

Experience the unique and rich flavor of our premium coffee beans, grown in the lush, volcanic soil of Guanacaste, Costa Rica. Our beans are carefully hand-picked by local farmers and expertly roasted to bring out their natural sweetness and complexity. With notes of chocolate, caramel, and citrus, our coffee is the perfect way to start your day or enjoy a relaxing afternoon.

Montecillos Blend

A shade-grown coffee from a protected natural reserve, with a smooth finish and notes of dark chocolate.

San Marcos Microlot

A rare, small-batch coffee from the San Marcos region, known for its complex flavor profile and bright acidity.

Tres Rios Specialty

A full-bodied coffee from a family-owned local farm, with a rich aroma and a hint of nutty flavours.

The beans are processed using traditional methods that have been passed down for centuries, to preserve the unique flavor and character of each batch. From the farm to the cup, our coffee is a true reflection of the hard work, dedication, and passion of the farmers who grow it.
