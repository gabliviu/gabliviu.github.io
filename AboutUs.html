This is Our Story

At CostaCafe, we are passionate about coffee and dedicated to bringing you the finest blends from the lush mountains of Costa Rica. Our beans are sourced directly from local farmers who use traditional growing methods passed down for generations. We believe that coffee is not just a drink, but a celebration of culture and community.

Our commitment to quality extends beyond the beans we source. Our roasting process is carefully crafted to bring out the unique flavors and aromas of each batch, ensuring that every cup of CostaCafe coffee is a true taste of Costa Rica.

We are proud to be a part of the thriving coffee industry in Costa Rica and to support the local economy by creating sustainable jobs and promoting environmentally conscious practices.

Whether you're a coffee connoisseur or simply enjoy a good cup of joe, we invite you to join us on a journey of discovery and taste the difference that passion and quality can make. Try a cup of CostaCafe coffee today and experience the rich heritage of Costa Rica in every sip.

Juan starts by carefully selecting only the ripest and finest beans from his farm. He then carefully roasts the beans in small batches, constantly monitoring the temperature and time to ensure that each bean is roasted to perfection. He uses a traditional drum roaster, which allows for a slow and controlled roast, bringing out the unique flavors and aromas of each bean.
